# PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT GUIDELINES

## COMMUNICATION PROTOCOLS
- **Email Response Time:** Within 24 hours for internal, 48 hours for external
- **Meeting Punctuality:** Arrive 5 minutes early for all meetings
- **Digital Communication:** Professional language in all channels
- **Availability Status:** Clear communication of availability and response times

## MEETING ETIQUETTE
- Come prepared with agenda review completed
- Active participation without dominating discussion
- Respectful listening to all contributors
- Action item documentation and follow-through

## TEAM COLLABORATION
- Respect for diverse working styles and perspectives
- Constructive feedback focused on solutions
- Credit sharing and acknowledgment of contributions
- Proactive communication of challenges and needs

## DIGITAL CITIZENSHIP
- Professional online presence maintenance
- Confidential information protection
- Appropriate use of company resources
- Ethical technology use principles
