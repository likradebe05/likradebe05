# TEAM COLLABORATION AGREEMENT

## COMMUNICATION STANDARDS
- Daily stand-ups at 9:00 AM (15 minutes max)
- Slack for urgent matters, email for formal communications
- 4-hour response time expectation during work hours

## CONFLICT RESOLUTION
- Address issues within 24 hours of identification
- Focus on problem-solving, not personal criticism
- Escalate unresolved issues after 2 attempts at resolution

## WORKFLOW MANAGEMENT
- GitHub project board for task tracking
- Weekly progress reviews every Friday
- Clear definition of "done" for each task type

## SUCCESS METRICS
- On-time delivery of milestones
- Code quality standards maintained
- Team satisfaction scores
- Client/stakeholder feedback
